Research in

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Dr. Michael Harhay and Dr. Bo Zhang (Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center). My work develops rigorous statistical methodology for causal inference in observational studies, randomized trials, and vaccine immunology.
