Zhe Chen

Research-Track Assistant Professor (starting July 2026)
Department of Biostatistics, Epidemiology & Informatics · University of Pennsylvania

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Dr. Michael Harhay and Dr. Bo Zhang (Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center). My research develops rigorous statistical methodology for causal inference in observational studies, randomized trials, and vaccine immunology. I will join Penn DBEI as a research-track assistant professor in July 2026.
